Common questions

How do I delete my account? Open the app, go to Profile → Settings → Delete account. All your questions, votes, comments, photos, and friend connections are removed within seven days.

I'm not getting push notifications. Check iOS Settings → Notifications → YayOrNay and make sure alerts are allowed. Inside the app, go to Profile → Settings → Notifications to toggle individual categories (new question, new vote, new comment, mentions, friend requests).

How do I block or report someone? Tap any user's profile to block them, or open a question and tap the more menu to report it. Blocks hide that user from your feeds and comments. Reported questions are reviewed and auto-hidden if they exceed a report threshold.

I signed in with Apple and want to change my email. Apple generates a private relay email for you. To change it, go to appleid.apple.com → Sign in with Apple → YayOrNay and update your forwarding address.
